Microcontroller Snacks Moderator
❮ ❯
AUG. 14, 2018

During this project, we were tasked to incorporate a microcontroller chip (PIC18F4550), to create a device that will help others. Through our ideation process, we came up with various ideas.

We finally decided to create a snack moderator since it had the ability to serve a wide range of users and help more people. The project was completed over the duration of the module.
